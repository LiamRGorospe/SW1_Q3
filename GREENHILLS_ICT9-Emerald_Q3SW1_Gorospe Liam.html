Synopsis

Saul Goodman is a lawyer who helps people, even if they are clearly guilty, he will use tricks to prove the guilty innocent. He is the Protoganist of the show thus Better Call Saul. Saul Goodman can also be referred to as Jimmy McGill

This show is a spinoff to the series breaking bad as we see the origins of Saul Goodman. He played a big part for the two characters Walter White and Jesse Pinkman incase they needed to get out of some illegal problems
